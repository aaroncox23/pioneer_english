English is all around us, but we know how difficult it can be if you don’t understand. That’s why we have developed a range of basic English courses for people to begin using English confidently. Assess Your Level

Get your English level assessed with our online and 1-2-1 teacher tests.

At the end of each test your level will be assessed by our professional teachers at CEFR levels. There are 6 levels in total, A1 to C2.

IELTS / TOEFL / TOEIC / KET / PET

These scores measure the ability of non-native speakers of English to use and understand as it is spoken, written and heard in college and university settings. Most people who take this path are planning to study at colleges and universities where the course is predominantly in English. In addition to this, many government bodies, scholarship programs, and licensing/certification agencies use these scores to evaluate English proficiency.
